Heart Rate Monitor

iOS mobile app to measure the heart rate of the subject using only the camera on an iPhone. The app senses the PPG (photoplethysmogram) signal of the subject by analyzing light intensity of every frame captured from the camera and reports the computed heart rate. Take a look at the working demo and measurement accuracy of the app.
